Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about inheritance cash advances and how Cash4ProbateHomes works.

Is this a loan?

No. This is an inheritance advance — you are accessing funds that are already rightfully yours as a named heir. There is no repayment, no interest, and no credit check required. Unlike a loan, you don't need to worry about monthly payments or debt collection.

How much can I get?

Your advance amount depends on the value of your inheritance. The more you stand to inherit, the larger your advance can be. Generally, you can access up to a portion of your expected inheritance value. Contact us for a free, no-obligation evaluation to find out exactly how much you qualify for.

How fast can I receive funds?

In many cases, funds can be available in as little as 24 hours after eligibility is confirmed. The overall timeline depends on how quickly we can verify your probate case information. Simple cases with clear documentation may be processed even faster.

Do I need good credit?

No. We do not check credit scores, employment history, or income. Your eligibility is based solely on your status as a named heir or beneficiary and the value of your inheritance. Even with bad credit or no income, you may still qualify.

What if I don't live in the same state as the probate case?

That's no problem at all. We handle probate cases across all 50 states. Your physical location has no bearing on your eligibility. Whether you live next door to the probate court or across the country, you can still receive your inheritance advance.

What types of inheritance qualify?

We primarily fund advances based on inherited real estate property — houses, condos, land, and commercial properties that are part of a probate estate. Cash or financial inheritances from estates may also qualify. Contact us to discuss your specific situation — we can tell you quickly whether you're eligible.

Is there any risk or obligation to apply?

Zero. The initial consultation and evaluation are completely free with absolutely no obligation. If we determine you qualify and you decide to move forward, there is still no repayment risk because this is your own inheritance — not a loan.

What if the probate case takes longer than expected?

That's exactly why inheritance advances exist. Probate can take months or even years to resolve. We provide you cash now so you don't have to wait. There is nothing to repay regardless of how long the probate process takes — the advance is settled when the estate distributes.

What information do I need to apply?

You'll need basic information about yourself (name, email, phone), the inherited property address, the estimated inheritance amount, and details about the probate case status. Don't have all the details yet? Apply with what you know and we can fill in the gaps together.

How is this different from selling my inheritance?

With an inheritance advance, you keep your inherited property — you're simply accessing some of its value early. Selling your inheritance (assigning your beneficiary rights) means giving up your interest entirely. Our advance allows you to keep your property while getting cash when you need it most.

Cash4ProbateHomes is not a lender. Cash4ProbateHomes provides immediate cash advances to heirs who are waiting for their inheritance in exchange for a fixed assignment of the heir's inheritance. These advances are not loans, with no interest rates, no collateral and no recourse if the heir's inheritance is insufficient to repay the advance. Cash4ProbateHomes bears all the risks inherent with the probate process, including, but not limited to, the value of the heir's inheritance due to devaluation of estate assets, creditor claims and other potential legal challenges to the probate.
