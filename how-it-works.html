How It Works

Getting your inheritance cash is simple. Three easy steps from application to funding — no credit check, no repayment, no hidden fees.

1

Apply Online — 5 Minutes

Complete our quick, secure application form. Tell us about yourself, the inherited property, and the probate case. No paperwork to print, no appointments to schedule, and no upfront fees — ever.

We only ask for the information we need to evaluate your case quickly. Everything is encrypted and handled confidentially.

2

Case Review & Verification

Our team researches your probate case to confirm your eligibility as an heir and verify the value of your inheritance. We handle all the legwork — contacting the probate court, reviewing case documents, and assessing the property value.

This step is typically completed within 24 to 48 hours. We keep you informed every step of the way with clear, regular updates.

3

Receive Your Cash Advance

Once your eligibility is confirmed and the case is verified, we send your inheritance cash advance — often in as little as 24 hours. The funds are deposited directly into your account.

Important: This is not a loan. There is no repayment required because you are accessing money that is rightfully yours. No interest, no monthly payments, no debt collection.

Cash4ProbateHomes is not a lender. Cash4ProbateHomes provides immediate cash advances to heirs who are waiting for their inheritance in exchange for a fixed assignment of the heir's inheritance. These advances are not loans, with no interest rates, no collateral and no recourse if the heir's inheritance is insufficient to repay the advance. Cash4ProbateHomes bears all the risks inherent with the probate process, including, but not limited to, the value of the heir's inheritance due to devaluation of estate assets, creditor claims and other potential legal challenges to the probate.
