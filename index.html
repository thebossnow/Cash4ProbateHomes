No. This is an inheritance advance — you are accessing funds that are already rightfully yours. There is no repayment required.

How much can I get?
Your advance amount depends on the value of your inheritance. Generally, you can access up to a portion of your expected inheritance.

Cash4ProbateHomes is not a lender. Cash4ProbateHomes provides immediate cash advances to heirs who are waiting for their inheritance in exchange for a fixed assignment of the heir's inheritance. Cash4ProbateHomes bears all the risks inherent with the probate process, including, but not limited to, the value of the heir's inheritance due to devaluation of estate assets, creditor claims and other potential legal challenges to the probate.
